# Transcription Termination

## Summary
Final stage of [[processes/transcription]]. At the **[[structures/terminator]]** sequence, [[enzymes/rna-polymerase]], the new RNA, and the DNA all separate. The DNA re-forms a continuous double helix. The new RNA undergoes [[processes/rna-processing]] in the nucleus, then exits to the cytoplasm.
