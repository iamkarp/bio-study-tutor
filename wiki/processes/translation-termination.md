# Translation Termination

## Summary
When the [[structures/ribosome]] reaches a **stop codon** (UAA, UAG, or UGA), no [[molecules/trna]] matches it. A **release factor** binds the empty A site instead, triggering release of the finished polypeptide and dissociation of the ribosome's two subunits.
