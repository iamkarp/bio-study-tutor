# PCR — Annealing

## Summary
Second step of each [[techniques/pcr]] cycle. The mixture is cooled to **~55 °C** so [[molecules/primer|primers]] can **base-pair (anneal)** to their complementary sequences on the now-separated DNA strands. Each primer is short (~20 nt) and is designed to flank one end of the target sequence — one primer binds each strand.
