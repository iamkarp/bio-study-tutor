# Prophase II

## Summary
The first stage of meiosis II. Chromosomes recondense (if they had decondensed in telophase I), the spindle re-forms, and microtubules capture the duplicated chromosomes. **Kinetochores of sister chromatids attach to opposite poles** (in contrast to metaphase I, where each whole chromosome attached to one pole). **No crossing over** at this stage.
