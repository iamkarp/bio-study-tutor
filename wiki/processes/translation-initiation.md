# Translation Initiation

## Summary
The **small ribosomal subunit** binds the 5' cap of [[molecules/mrna]] and scans until it finds the **start codon (AUG)**. The **initiator tRNA** carrying [[molecules/methionine]] base-pairs (anticodon 3'-UAC-5') with AUG. Then the **large ribosomal subunit** joins to complete the [[structures/ribosome]] with the initiator tRNA in the **P site**.
