# Translation Elongation

## Summary
A second [[molecules/trna]] enters the [[structures/ribosome]]'s **A site** with its anticodon matching the next codon on [[molecules/mrna]]. The ribosome's catalytic site (rRNA) **forms a peptide bond** transferring methionine onto the second tRNA's amino acid. The first (now empty) tRNA exits the **E site**; the ribosome **shifts one codon** down, moving the second tRNA from A → P site, freeing the A site for the next tRNA. Repeat for every codon.
