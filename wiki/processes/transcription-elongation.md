# Transcription Elongation

## Summary
[[enzymes/rna-polymerase]] moves along the **template strand** of DNA, reading 3' → 5', and adds RNA nucleotides to the **3' end** of the growing RNA strand (synthesis is 5' → 3'). Each incoming RNA nucleotide pairs with the template by complementary base-pairing (DNA's A → RNA's U, T → A, G → C, C → G). **Only one** DNA strand is copied.
