# Telophase II

## Summary
Cytokinesis occurs, nuclear membranes re-form, and chromosomes decondense. **Four haploid cells** result from one diploid starting cell, each with **n single chromosomes**. In humans, the four cells are gametes (or in females, one egg plus polar bodies).
