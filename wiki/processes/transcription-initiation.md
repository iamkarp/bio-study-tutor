# Transcription Initiation

## Summary
The first stage of [[processes/transcription]]. **[[enzymes/rna-polymerase]] binds to the [[structures/promoter]]** at the start of a gene. In eukaryotes, **[[structures/transcription-factor|transcription factors]] must bind first** to recruit RNA polymerase. Once bound, RNA polymerase locally **unwinds the DNA double helix** to expose the template strand.
