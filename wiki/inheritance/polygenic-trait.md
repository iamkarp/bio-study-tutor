# Polygenic Trait

## Summary
A trait controlled by **multiple genes** (often ≥3), each contributing a small additive effect. Polygenic traits show **continuous variation** across a population — e.g. **skin color**, height, weight, eye color (partly), blood pressure. Distribution is typically a bell curve rather than discrete categories.

Often modulated further by **environment** (see [[concepts/environment-and-phenotype]]).
