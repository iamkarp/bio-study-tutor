# DNA Ligase

## Summary
**Seals the gaps between DNA fragments** by catalyzing the formation of a **phosphodiester bond** between an existing 3'-OH and an adjacent 5'-phosphate. On the [[structures/lagging-strand]], ligase joins [[structures/okazaki-fragment|Okazaki fragments]] into a continuous strand after [[enzymes/dna-polymerase-i]] replaces the RNA primers.
