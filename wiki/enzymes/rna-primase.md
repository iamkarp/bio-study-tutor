# RNA Primase

## Summary
Synthesizes a **short RNA primer** (~5–10 nucleotides) on the template DNA. Unlike DNA polymerase, primase **can start a strand from scratch** — no pre-existing 3'-OH required. The primer it leaves behind provides the free 3'-OH that DNA polymerase then extends. Primers are later replaced with DNA by [[enzymes/dna-polymerase-i]].
