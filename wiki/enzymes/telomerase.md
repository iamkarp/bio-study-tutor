# Telomerase

## Summary
A specialized enzyme (a reverse transcriptase) that **adds repetitive DNA to the 3' end of telomeres**, compensating for the shortening that occurs each round of replication. Telomerase **carries its own RNA template** that it uses as a guide.

**Active in**: germ cells, stem cells, ~85% of cancer cells.

**Inactive in**: most adult somatic cells — which is why somatic cells have a finite division limit.
