# DNA Polymerase III

## Summary
The **main enzyme of DNA replication** — adds new nucleotides to the 3' end of a primer or growing strand, building DNA in the 5' → 3' direction. Has **3' → 5' exonuclease (proofreading)** activity that catches mismatches: error rate drops from ~1 per 10³ nt to ~1 per 10⁷ nt after proofreading. Cannot start a chain from scratch — requires a primer.
