# RNA Polymerase

## Summary
The enzyme that **synthesizes RNA from a DNA template** during [[processes/transcription]]. Binds the [[structures/promoter]] (in eukaryotes, only after [[structures/transcription-factor|transcription factors]] bind), unwinds the DNA, and reads the template 3' → 5' while building RNA 5' → 3'. Releases the RNA at the [[structures/terminator]].

Eukaryotes have three distinct RNA polymerases: Pol I (rRNA), Pol II (mRNA), Pol III (tRNA and other small RNAs). Prokaryotes have one.
