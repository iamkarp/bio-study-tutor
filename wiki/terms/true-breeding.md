# True Breeding (Pure Breeding)

## Summary
A line of organisms whose **self-fertilized offspring all show the same trait** as the parent. True-breeding individuals are **[[terms/homozygous|homozygous]]** for the gene of interest. **"Pure breeding" = "true breeding"** — they're synonymous. Mendel started his experiments by establishing true-breeding lines.
