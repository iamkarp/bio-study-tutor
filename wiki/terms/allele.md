# Allele

## Summary
An **alternative version of a gene**. Diploid cells carry two alleles per gene (one on each homologous chromosome); they may be the same ([[terms/homozygous|homozygous]]) or different ([[terms/heterozygous|heterozygous]]). Alleles can be [[terms/dominant|dominant]] (effect appears in heterozygotes) or [[terms/recessive|recessive]] (effect masked when a dominant is present).
