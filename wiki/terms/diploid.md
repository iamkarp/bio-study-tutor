# Diploid (2n)

## Summary
A cell with **two copies of each chromosome** (two sets, denoted **2n**) — one inherited from each parent. Human diploid cells (somatic cells) have **46 chromosomes** (2n=46). Most body cells are diploid; only gametes are haploid.
