# DNA vs. RNA

## Comparison

| Feature | DNA | RNA |
|---|---|---|
| **Sugar** | Deoxyribose | Ribose (has 2'-OH) |
| **Bases** | A, T, G, C | A, **U** (uracil), G, C |
| **Strands** | Double-stranded helix | Single-stranded |
| **Length** | Very long (entire chromosomes) | Much shorter |
| **Lifespan** | Stable, persists for cell's lifetime | Transient — usually degraded after use |
| **Location (eukaryote)** | Nucleus (mostly) | Made in nucleus, used in cytoplasm |
| **Function** | Long-term genetic storage | Diverse: messenger, structural (rRNA), adaptor (tRNA), regulatory (miRNA) |
