# Chapter 9 — Sexual Reproduction and Meiosis

## Summary
Chapter 9 introduces sexual reproduction as a source of genetic diversity, contrasts autosomes with sex chromosomes (XX female / XY male), then walks through meiosis: one round of DNA replication followed by two rounds of cell division (meiosis I and meiosis II) that produce four haploid gametes. Crossing over and independent assortment generate variability; nondisjunction errors cause chromosomal disorders.
