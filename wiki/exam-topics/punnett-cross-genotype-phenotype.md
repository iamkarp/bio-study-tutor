# Drawing F1 and F2 from a P cross (worked example: TT × tt)

## Answer
Let T = dominant tall allele; t = recessive short allele.

**P cross**: TT × tt (both true-breeding).

**F1**: TT × tt produces only Tt offspring.

|  | T | T |
|---|---|---|
| **t** | Tt | Tt |
| **t** | Tt | Tt |

→ F1 genotype: **100% Tt** (heterozygous). Phenotype: **100% tall** (dominant masks recessive).

**F2** (Tt × Tt — F1 self-cross):

|  | T | t |
|---|---|---|
| **T** | TT | Tt |
| **t** | Tt | tt |

→ F2 genotype ratio: **1 TT : 2 Tt : 1 tt**. Phenotype ratio: **3 tall : 1 short**.
