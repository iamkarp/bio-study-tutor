# When does DNA replication occur?

## Answer
DNA replication occurs during the **S phase ("synthesis" phase) of interphase**, which is part of the **cell cycle**:
- **G1** — first growth phase
- **S** — DNA replication ← _here_
- **G2** — second growth phase
- **M** — mitosis (or meiosis)

By the end of S phase, every chromosome has been copied and consists of two **sister chromatids** held together at the centromere.
