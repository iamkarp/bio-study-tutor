# What does "pure breeding" mean?

## Answer
**Pure breeding** (= **true breeding**) means a plant or animal whose self-fertilized offspring **all show the same trait as the parent**. Pure-breeding individuals are **homozygous** for the gene(s) of interest (YY or yy, never Yy). Mendel started his experiments by establishing pure-breeding lines so he could be confident about the genotypes of his P generation.
