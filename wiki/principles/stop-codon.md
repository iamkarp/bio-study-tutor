# Stop Codon

## Summary
The three codons **UAA, UAG, UGA** that **end translation**. They do not code for any amino acid. When the ribosome reaches a stop codon, **no tRNA matches it**; instead a **release factor** binds and triggers release of the finished polypeptide.

Mnemonic: "**U-A-A** = U Are Away; **U-A-G** = U Are Gone; **U-G-A** = U Go Away."
