# Sex Chromosomes

## Summary
The pair of chromosomes that determine biological sex in mammals. Humans have one pair (the 23rd pair):
- **[[structures/x-chromosome|XX]]** = female
- **[[structures/x-chromosome|X]][[structures/y-chromosome|Y]]** = male

Sex-linked traits ([[inheritance/sex-linked]]) are carried on these chromosomes — most often the X (which has many genes) rather than the much smaller Y.
