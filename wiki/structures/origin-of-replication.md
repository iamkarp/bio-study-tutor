# Origin of Replication (ORI)

## Summary
A specific DNA sequence where DNA replication starts. Helicase opens the helix here, creating a [[structures/replication-bubble]] with two outward-moving [[structures/replication-fork|replication forks]].

| | Prokaryotes | Eukaryotes |
|---|---|---|
| Number of ORIs | **One** per circular chromosome | **Many** per linear chromosome |
| Why | Genome is small (~few Mb) and circular | Chromosomes are huge (up to ~250 Mb) — one ORI couldn't finish in time |
