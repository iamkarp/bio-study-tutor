# X Chromosome

## Summary
The larger of the two human sex chromosomes, carrying ~800 genes. **Females (XX) have two copies; males (XY) have one.** Many sex-linked recessive disorders (color blindness, hemophilia) reside on the X — males are more susceptible because they have no second X to mask a recessive allele. See [[concepts/x-inactivation]].
