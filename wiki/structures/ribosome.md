# Ribosome

## Summary
The **molecular machine that translates mRNA into protein**. Composed of [[molecules/rrna|rRNA]] and proteins, organized into a **large subunit** and a **small subunit** that come together around an mRNA.

- **Small subunit** — binds [[molecules/mrna]].
- **Large subunit** — binds [[molecules/trna|tRNAs]] in three sites: **A** (aminoacyl, incoming), **P** (peptidyl, growing chain), **E** (exit).

The ribosome's **catalytic activity is carried out by rRNA** (it's a ribozyme), specifically the peptidyl transferase center in the large subunit, which forms peptide bonds between amino acids.

Multiple ribosomes can simultaneously translate the same mRNA — a structure called a **polyribosome**.
