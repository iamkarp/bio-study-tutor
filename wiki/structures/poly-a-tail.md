# 3' Poly-A Tail

## Summary
A long string of **adenine nucleotides (~100–250 in eukaryotes)** added to the 3' end of pre-mRNA during processing. Functions:
- **Stabilizes** the mRNA against degradation.
- **Aids nuclear export** to the cytoplasm.
- Tail length correlates with mRNA lifespan; tails are gradually trimmed and once they're too short the mRNA is destroyed.
