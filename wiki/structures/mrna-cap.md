# 5' mRNA Cap

## Summary
A **modified guanine nucleotide** added to the 5' end of pre-mRNA during processing. Functions:
- **Recognition signal** for the small ribosomal subunit during [[processes/translation-initiation]].
- **Protects** the mRNA from being degraded by exonucleases.
