# Chromosome

## Summary
A long, organized molecule of DNA wrapped around histone proteins. Humans have **46 chromosomes** (23 pairs) in each diploid somatic cell. Before division, each chromosome is duplicated into two **[[structures/sister-chromatids]]** held at the centromere.

## Types
- **[[structures/autosome|Autosomes]]** — chromosomes 1–22; same for both sexes.
- **[[structures/sex-chromosomes]]** — pair 23: XX (female) or XY (male).

## Eukaryote vs. Prokaryote
- **Eukaryotes**: linear, multiple per cell, packaged with histones in a nucleus.
- **Prokaryotes**: typically one large circular chromosome plus optional [[structures/plasmid|plasmids]].
