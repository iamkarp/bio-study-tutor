# Sugar–Phosphate Backbone

## Summary
The **outer scaffold of a DNA strand**, formed by alternating [[molecules/deoxyribose|deoxyribose]] sugars and [[structures/phosphate-group|phosphate groups]] linked by **[[structures/phosphodiester-bond|phosphodiester bonds]]**. Faces the **exterior** of the [[structures/double-helix]]. The bases project inward from the backbone and pair with bases on the opposite strand.
