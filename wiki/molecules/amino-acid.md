# Amino Acid

## Summary
The **building block of proteins**. There are **20 standard amino acids** used to build all proteins in living things. Each amino acid is encoded by one or more codons via the [[principles/genetic-code]]. Amino acids are joined together by **peptide bonds** during [[processes/translation-elongation]].
