# rRNA — Ribosomal RNA

## Summary
The structural and **catalytic RNA component of the [[structures/ribosome]]**. rRNA forms the bulk of the ribosome's mass and catalyzes peptide bond formation between amino acids during translation — making the ribosome a **ribozyme** (an RNA enzyme).
