# Deoxyribose

## Summary
The **5-carbon sugar** in a DNA nucleotide. "Deoxy" means it lacks an –OH at the 2' position (compared to ribose, which has it). The carbons are numbered 1' through 5':
- **5' carbon** — attached to the [[structures/phosphate-group]].
- **3' carbon** — has the –OH that gets linked to the next nucleotide's phosphate (this is what DNA polymerase extends).
- **1' carbon** — attached to the [[structures/nitrogenous-base]].
