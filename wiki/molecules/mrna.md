# mRNA — Messenger RNA

## Summary
The RNA that carries the **genetic message from DNA to the ribosome**. Made by [[enzymes/rna-polymerase]] during [[processes/transcription]]. In eukaryotes, mRNA is processed (5' cap, poly-A tail, splicing) before exiting the nucleus. At the ribosome, mRNA is read in **codons** (3 nucleotides) to specify the amino acid sequence of a protein.
