# RNA — Ribonucleic Acid

## Summary
A nucleic acid similar to DNA but with three differences:
1. **Sugar** is **ribose** (has 2'-OH); DNA's is deoxyribose.
2. **Base** uracil (U) replaces thymine (T).
3. RNA is typically **single-stranded**.

Three main classes: [[molecules/mrna]] (messenger), [[molecules/trna]] (transfer), [[molecules/rrna]] (ribosomal). Other types: [[molecules/microrna]], snRNA (splicing), etc.
