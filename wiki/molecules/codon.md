# Codon

## Summary
A **3-nucleotide sequence** on [[molecules/mrna]] that specifies one amino acid (or a stop signal). There are 4³ = **64 possible codons**, of which **61 specify amino acids** (with redundancy — most amino acids have multiple codons, see [[concepts/codon-redundancy]]) and **3 are stop codons** (UAA, UAG, UGA). The **start codon AUG** also codes for [[molecules/methionine]].

A codon is recognized by the **anticodon** of a matching [[molecules/trna]].
