# DNA — Deoxyribonucleic Acid

## Summary
The molecule that stores the information cells need to make proteins. DNA is a **double-stranded helix** ([[structures/double-helix]]) of nucleotides. Each nucleotide consists of a [[structures/phosphate-group]], a [[molecules/deoxyribose]] sugar, and one of four [[structures/nitrogenous-base|nitrogenous bases]]: [[molecules/adenine|A]], [[molecules/thymine|T]], [[molecules/guanine|G]], [[molecules/cytosine|C]].
