# Anticodon

## Summary
The **3-nucleotide sequence on [[molecules/trna]]** that base-pairs with a complementary [[molecules/codon]] on mRNA. The anticodon-codon pairing brings the **correct amino acid** (carried at the tRNA's other end) to the ribosome. Pairing follows the standard rules: A↔U, G↔C (with some flexibility at the 3rd position — "wobble").
