# Insertion Mutation

## Summary
A mutation in which **one or more bases are added** to the DNA sequence. If the insertion is **a multiple of 3** (3, 6, 9, …), it adds whole codons but doesn't shift the reading frame — only adds extra amino acids to the protein. If it's **not a multiple of 3**, it causes a [[concepts/frameshift-mutation|frameshift]] and is usually catastrophic.
