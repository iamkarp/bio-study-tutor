# Gene Linkage

## Summary
Genes located **close together on the same chromosome** are **linked** — they tend to be inherited together rather than assorting independently. This is an **exception to** [[principles/law-of-independent-assortment]].

How tightly two genes are linked depends on **distance**: the closer they are, the less likely a [[processes/crossing-over]] event will separate them. Geneticists use crossover frequency (in centimorgans) to map gene positions.
