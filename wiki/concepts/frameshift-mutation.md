# Frameshift Mutation

## Summary
An [[concepts/insertion-mutation|insertion]] or [[concepts/deletion-mutation|deletion]] of a number of bases **not a multiple of 3**. Because codons are read in groups of three, the reading frame shifts after the mutation site, and **every codon downstream changes**. The protein after the shift is essentially gibberish, and a premature stop codon often appears soon after — producing a **truncated, non-functional protein**. Usually catastrophic.
