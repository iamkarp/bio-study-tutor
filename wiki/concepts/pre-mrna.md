# Pre-mRNA

## Summary
The **initial transcript** produced by RNA polymerase from a eukaryotic gene — **longer than the final message** because it contains both [[structures/intron|introns]] and [[structures/exon|exons]]. Before it can leave the nucleus and be translated, pre-mRNA undergoes [[processes/rna-processing]]:
1. 5' cap added ([[structures/mrna-cap]])
2. 3' poly-A tail added ([[structures/poly-a-tail]])
3. [[processes/splicing|Introns spliced out]], exons joined

The result is **mature mRNA**, which exits the nucleus to be translated in the cytoplasm.
