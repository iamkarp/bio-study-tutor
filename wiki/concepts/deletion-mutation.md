# Deletion Mutation

## Summary
A mutation in which **one or more bases are removed** from the DNA sequence. Multiple-of-3 deletions remove whole amino acids without shifting the frame; non-multiple-of-3 deletions cause a [[concepts/frameshift-mutation|frameshift]] downstream.
