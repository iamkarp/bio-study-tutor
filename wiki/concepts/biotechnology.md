# Biotechnology

## Summary
The use of **living organisms or their molecular components** (DNA, RNA, proteins) to make useful products or carry out useful processes. Includes:
- **Diagnostic tools** — [[techniques/pcr]], [[techniques/dna-sequencing]], DNA fingerprinting
- **Therapeutic** — [[concepts/gene-therapy]], recombinant insulin, vaccines
- **Agricultural** — GMO crops, selective breeding
- **Forensic** — [[techniques/str-analysis]]
- **Environmental** — bioremediation

[[concepts/genetic-engineering]] is a subset of biotechnology focused on directly modifying genomes.
