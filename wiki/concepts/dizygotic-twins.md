# Dizygotic Twins

## Summary
**Fraternal twins** — two **separate sperm** fertilize **two separate egg cells** in the same cycle. Genetically, dizygotic twins are no more similar than ordinary siblings (~50% shared DNA). They can be different sexes and look very different.
