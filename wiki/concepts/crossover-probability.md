# Crossover Probability

## Summary
The **probability of [[processes/crossing-over]] occurring between two genes is proportional to the distance between them** on the chromosome. Genes far apart cross over often; closely linked genes cross over rarely. This is the basis for **genetic mapping** — frequencies of recombination are translated into distances (centimorgans).
