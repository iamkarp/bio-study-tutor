# Mutation

## Summary
A **permanent change in the DNA base sequence**. Most mutations occur during replication (despite proofreading) or as a result of exposure to a [[terms/mutagen]]. Mutations are the ultimate source of all genetic variation — most are neutral or harmful, but a small fraction are beneficial and provide the raw material for evolution.

## Categories
- [[concepts/point-mutation]] — single base change
- [[concepts/substitution-mutation]] — one base swapped (silent / missense / nonsense)
- [[concepts/insertion-mutation]] — base(s) added
- [[concepts/deletion-mutation]] — base(s) removed
- [[concepts/frameshift-mutation]] — insertion/deletion not a multiple of 3 → reading frame shifts
