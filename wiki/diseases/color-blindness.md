# Red-Green Color Blindness

## Summary
An **X-linked recessive** trait — inability to distinguish red from green wavelengths of light. Affects about **8% of males** but only ~0.5% of females (because females need two copies of the recessive allele). Caused by mutations in the OPN1LW or OPN1MW opsin genes on the X chromosome.
