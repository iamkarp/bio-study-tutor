# Central Dogma

## Summary
The flow of genetic information in cells:

> **DNA → RNA → Protein**

- **DNA → RNA**: [[processes/transcription]] (in the nucleus) catalyzed by [[enzymes/rna-polymerase]]
- **RNA → Protein**: [[processes/translation]] (at ribosomes in the cytoplasm) catalyzed by the [[structures/ribosome]]

Originally articulated by Francis Crick. Modern revisions include reverse transcription (RNA → DNA, e.g. retroviruses) and RNA replication, but the core flow holds.
