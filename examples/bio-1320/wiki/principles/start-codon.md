# Start Codon — AUG

## Summary
The codon **AUG** marks the **beginning of translation** on an mRNA. AUG also encodes [[molecules/methionine]], so **every protein begins with methionine**. The initiator tRNA carrying methionine binds AUG with its anticodon (3'-UAC-5') during [[processes/translation-initiation]].

AUG codons internal to a gene also specify methionine — they're not "start" signals there, just regular methionine codons.
