# microRNA (miRNA)

## Summary
A short (~22 nt) non-coding RNA that **regulates gene expression** at the mRNA level. miRNAs base-pair with complementary sequences on mRNAs and either **block translation** or trigger mRNA degradation. They are a major mechanism of post-transcriptional gene regulation in eukaryotes.
