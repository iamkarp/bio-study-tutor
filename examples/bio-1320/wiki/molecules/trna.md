# tRNA — Transfer RNA

## Summary
A small (~76 nucleotide) RNA that acts as the **adaptor** between mRNA codons and amino acids. Each tRNA has:
- An **anticodon** that base-pairs with a specific [[molecules/codon]] on mRNA.
- An **amino acid attached at its 3' end**, matched to the anticodon (loaded by aminoacyl-tRNA synthetases).

The **initiator tRNA** carries [[molecules/methionine]] and recognizes the AUG **start codon**. Stop codons have **no matching tRNA** — that's why they trigger [[processes/translation-termination]].
