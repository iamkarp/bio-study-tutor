# Dihybrid Cross

## Summary
A genetic cross **tracking two genes** simultaneously (e.g. seed color × seed shape). For two heterozygous parents (AaBb × AaBb), the F2 generation shows the classic **9:3:3:1 phenotypic ratio**:
- 9 dominant–dominant
- 3 dominant–recessive
- 3 recessive–dominant
- 1 recessive–recessive

This pattern is the empirical basis for [[principles/law-of-independent-assortment]]. The exam will not ask you to map out a dihybrid cross.
