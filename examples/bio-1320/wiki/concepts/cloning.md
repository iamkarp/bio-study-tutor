# Cloning

## Summary
Producing **genetically identical copies** of DNA, cells, or organisms.
- **Gene/molecular cloning** — copy a specific DNA segment, often via [[techniques/pcr]] or [[concepts/recombinant-dna]] vectors.
- **Cell cloning** — grow many cells from a single ancestor.
- **Reproductive cloning** — produce a whole organism genetically identical to the donor (e.g. Dolly the sheep, 1996, by somatic cell nuclear transfer).

Used in research, medicine (stem cells), and agriculture.
