# Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD)

## Summary
A technique used during in vitro fertilization (IVF) to **screen embryos for genetic disorders before implantation**. A single cell is removed from the embryo at the 8-cell stage; its DNA is amplified by [[techniques/pcr]] and tested with **DNA probes** for specific genetic conditions (cystic fibrosis, Huntington's, etc.). Only embryos free of the disorder are implanted.
