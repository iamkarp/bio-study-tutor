# Recombinant DNA

## Summary
DNA composed of segments **from different sources joined together**. Created in the lab — for example, splicing a human insulin gene into a bacterial [[structures/plasmid]] so the bacterium produces human insulin. One of the first major tools of [[concepts/genetic-engineering]].
