# Short Tandem Repeat (STR)

## Summary
A region of DNA where a **short sequence (2–6 bp) is repeated many times in a row** — e.g. AGAT-AGAT-AGAT-…. The **number of repeats varies between individuals**, making STRs ideal markers for identification.

The FBI's CODIS panel uses **13 standard STR loci**. At each locus, a person inherits one count from each parent. Across all 13 loci, the chance of two unrelated people matching is ~1 in a trillion.
