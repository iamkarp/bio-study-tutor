# Heteropaternal Superfecundation

## Summary
A rare form of [[concepts/dizygotic-twins|dizygotic twins]] in which **two egg cells are fertilized by sperm from different fathers** during the same cycle. Still fraternal twins (two zygotes, two pregnancies in parallel) — just with two fathers.
