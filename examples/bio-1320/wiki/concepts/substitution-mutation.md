# Substitution Mutation

## Summary
A mutation in which **one base is swapped for another** (e.g. A→G, T→C). Effect on the protein depends on the codon:
- **Silent** — codon still codes for the same amino acid (thanks to [[concepts/codon-redundancy|codon redundancy]]) → no effect.
- **Missense** — codon now codes for a different amino acid → protein may have altered function ([[diseases/sickle-cell-anemia]] example).
- **Nonsense** — codon becomes a stop codon → truncated, often non-functional protein.
