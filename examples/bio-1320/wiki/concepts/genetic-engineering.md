# Genetic Engineering

## Summary
The **deliberate alteration of an organism's genome** — inserting, deleting, or modifying DNA sequences. A subset of [[concepts/biotechnology]]. Includes:
- **[[concepts/recombinant-dna]]** — combining DNA from different sources (e.g. inserting human insulin gene into bacteria)
- **CRISPR-Cas9** — modern targeted gene editing
- **Transgenic organisms** — GMO crops, knockout mice

Distinct from biotechnology in general because it specifically modifies genetic material rather than just using existing biological tools.
