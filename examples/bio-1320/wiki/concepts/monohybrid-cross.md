# Monohybrid Cross

## Summary
A genetic cross that **tracks one gene** (one trait) at a time, typically **Aa × Aa** between two heterozygotes. The classic monohybrid cross produces a **3:1 phenotypic ratio** in F2 (dominant : recessive) and a **1:2:1 genotype ratio** (AA : Aa : aa).

This is the only kind of Punnett square the exam will require.
