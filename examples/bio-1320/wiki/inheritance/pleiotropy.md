# Pleiotropy (as inheritance pattern)

## Summary
**One gene with multiple, seemingly unrelated phenotypic effects.** The effects can be in different organ systems and look unconnected at first glance. Marfan syndrome (fibrillin-1 mutation → tall stature, long fingers, lens dislocation, aortic aneurysm) is the canonical example. See also [[terms/pleiotropy]].
