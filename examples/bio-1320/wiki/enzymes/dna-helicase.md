# DNA Helicase

## Summary
**Unwinds the DNA double helix** at the [[structures/replication-fork]] by breaking the **hydrogen bonds** between complementary base pairs. Uses ATP. Helicase moves along the DNA, splitting the duplex into two single strands so they can be used as templates for replication.
