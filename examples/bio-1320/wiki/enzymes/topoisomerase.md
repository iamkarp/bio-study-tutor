# Topoisomerase

## Summary
**Relieves the twisting/supercoiling tension** that builds up ahead of the [[structures/replication-fork]] as helicase unwinds the DNA. Without topoisomerase, the DNA would become so twisted it would block replication. Topoisomerase works by transiently cutting the DNA backbone, allowing it to relax, then re-sealing.
