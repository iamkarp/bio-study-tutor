# Single-Stranded Binding Protein

## Summary
A protein that **coats single-stranded DNA** at the [[structures/replication-fork]] to prevent it from re-annealing into a double helix or forming hairpin secondary structures. Keeps the template accessible for [[enzymes/dna-polymerase-iii|DNA polymerase III]]. Strictly a "binding protein" — not catalytically an enzyme — but treated alongside enzymes in the replication context.
