# DNA Polymerase I

## Summary
The enzyme that **replaces RNA primers with DNA nucleotides** during DNA replication. Works in the 5' → 3' direction. After DNA polymerase I finishes, [[enzymes/dna-ligase|DNA ligase]] seals the remaining nick. Particularly important on the [[structures/lagging-strand]], which has many primers (one per [[structures/okazaki-fragment]]).
