# Telophase I

## Summary
Two haploid clusters of duplicated chromosomes form at opposite poles. Spindle microtubules disappear; cytokinesis usually occurs (cytoplasm divides). Nuclear envelopes may briefly re-form. **Chromosomes do NOT replicate between telophase I and prophase II.** Telophase I usually transitions immediately into meiosis II with little or no interphase.
