# Splicing

## Summary
The process of cutting [[structures/intron|introns]] out of a pre-mRNA and joining the remaining [[structures/exon|exons]] together to form a continuous mature mRNA. Performed by a complex called the **spliceosome** (made of small nuclear RNAs and proteins). Through [[concepts/alternative-splicing]], a single gene can yield multiple proteins by joining different combinations of exons.
