# Anaphase II

## Summary
**Sister chromatids separate** and move to opposite poles. Each chromatid is now a single (unduplicated) chromosome. If [[processes/nondisjunction]] occurs here (only in some of the cells from meiosis I), only some gametes will be abnormal — outcome: 1 with extra, 1 missing, 2 normal.
