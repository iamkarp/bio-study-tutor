# PCR — Denaturation

## Summary
First step of each [[techniques/pcr]] cycle. The mixture is heated to **~95 °C**, which **breaks the hydrogen bonds** between the two strands of DNA and separates them. Hydrogen bonds are easy to disrupt with heat (compared to covalent bonds in the backbone), so heating makes DNA single-stranded without destroying it.
