# Oogenesis

## Summary
Meiosis in females. A diploid oogonium undergoes asymmetric divisions: cytokinesis is unequal, so most of the cytoplasm goes to one daughter cell. Result: **one large egg (ovum) and 2–3 small polar bodies** (which usually die). Begins before birth and pauses; one egg per menstrual cycle finishes meiosis if fertilized.
