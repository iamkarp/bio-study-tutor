# Prophase I

## Summary
The first and longest phase of meiosis I. Homologous chromosomes pair up (synapsis), [[processes/crossing-over]] occurs at chiasmata, the nuclear envelope breaks down, and the spindle forms.

## Key Events
- **Synapsis** — homologous chromosomes pair tightly along their length.
- **Crossing over** — non-sister chromatids exchange segments at chiasmata, generating recombinant chromatids. Each pair typically forms 2–3 chiasmata.
- **Spindle assembly** — microtubules attach to kinetochores.
- **Nuclear envelope breakdown.**
