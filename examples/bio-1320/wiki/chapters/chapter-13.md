# Chapter 13 — The New Biology

## Summary
Chapter 13 surveys biotechnology: PCR amplifies DNA exponentially via cycles of denaturation/annealing/extension; gel electrophoresis separates DNA fragments by size; DNA sequencing reads the order of bases; STR profiling fingerprints individuals. The Human Genome Project sequenced all 3 billion bases of human DNA by 2003.
