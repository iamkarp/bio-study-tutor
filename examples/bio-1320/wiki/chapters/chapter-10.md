# Chapter 10 — Patterns of Inheritance

## Summary
Chapter 10 covers Mendelian genetics: dominant/recessive alleles, P/F1/F2 generations, monohybrid and dihybrid Punnett squares, and the laws of segregation and independent assortment. It then extends Mendel's framework to incomplete dominance, codominance (ABO blood typing), pleiotropy, sex-linked traits, X-inactivation, polygenic traits, gene linkage, and environmental influences on phenotype.
