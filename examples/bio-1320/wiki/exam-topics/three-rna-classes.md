# Three classes of RNA

## Answer

| RNA type | Function |
|---|---|
| **mRNA (messenger RNA)** | Carries the genetic message from DNA in the nucleus to a ribosome in the cytoplasm. The codon sequence on mRNA specifies the amino acid sequence of the protein. |
| **tRNA (transfer RNA)** | The "adaptor" that brings amino acids to the ribosome. Each tRNA carries one specific amino acid and has an **anticodon** that base-pairs with the matching codon on mRNA. |
| **rRNA (ribosomal RNA)** | A structural and catalytic component of the ribosome. rRNA forms the bulk of the ribosome and catalyzes peptide bond formation. |

All three are made by RNA polymerase via transcription, and all three cooperate during translation.
