# What are the sex chromosomes? What makes someone female vs. male?

## Answer
Humans have 23 pairs of chromosomes. **22 pairs are autosomes** (numbered 1–22) and **1 pair is sex chromosomes**.
- **XX = female**
- **XY = male**

The presence of a Y chromosome (carrying the SRY gene) drives male development; XX produces a female.
