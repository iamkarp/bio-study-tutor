# First amino acid in every protein

## Answer
Every protein starts with **methionine (Met)**, encoded by the **start codon AUG**. The initiator tRNA, carrying methionine, base-pairs with the AUG start codon during translation initiation. (Some proteins later have the initial methionine cleaved off post-translationally, but all begin synthesis with it.)
