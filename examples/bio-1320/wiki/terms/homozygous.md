# Homozygous

## Summary
Carrying **two identical alleles** for a given gene. Subtypes:
- **Homozygous dominant** (e.g. YY) — two dominant alleles.
- **Homozygous recessive** (e.g. yy) — two recessive alleles; this is when a recessive trait is expressed.

[[terms/true-breeding|True-breeding]] organisms are homozygous for the gene of interest.
