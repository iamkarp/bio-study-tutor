# Mutagen

## Summary
Any agent that **increases the rate of mutation** in DNA. Examples: UV light, X-rays, gamma radiation, tobacco smoke, certain industrial chemicals (benzene, formaldehyde), some viruses. Mutagens damage bases or the DNA backbone, leading to errors during replication.
