# Gene

## Summary
A **sequence of DNA that encodes a specific protein** (or a functional RNA, like tRNA/rRNA). The basic unit of heredity. Genes occupy specific positions ([[terms/locus|loci]]) on chromosomes; diploid cells have two copies of each gene (one on each homologous chromosome) which may be the same or different [[terms/allele|alleles]].
