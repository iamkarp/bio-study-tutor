# Polygenic

## Summary
A trait controlled by **multiple genes** — each contributing a small effect. Polygenic traits show **continuous variation** across a population (e.g. **skin color**, height, weight) rather than discrete categories. Often called "continuous" traits. See [[inheritance/polygenic-trait]].
