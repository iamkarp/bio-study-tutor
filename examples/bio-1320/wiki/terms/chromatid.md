# Chromatid

## Summary
**One of the two identical strands of a duplicated chromosome.** After DNA replication in S phase, each chromosome consists of **two sister chromatids** held together at the centromere. They are genetically identical (apart from possible crossover products in prophase I) and separate from each other during anaphase II of meiosis (or anaphase of mitosis).
