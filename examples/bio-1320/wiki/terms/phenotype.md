# Phenotype

## Summary
The **observable characteristics** of an organism (color, shape, behavior, biochemistry). Phenotype is the result of **genotype + environment** — see [[concepts/environment-and-phenotype]] (Siamese cat fur color is the classic example). For a gene with complete dominance, both YY and Yy give the same phenotype but different genotypes.
