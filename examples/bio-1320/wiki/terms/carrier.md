# Carrier

## Summary
A **heterozygous individual** who carries a recessive disease allele but **does not show the disease**. Two carriers (Aa × Aa) have a 25% chance of having an affected child (aa). For [[inheritance/x-linked-recessive]] traits, only **females can be carriers** — males have only one X, so any X-linked recessive allele is expressed.
