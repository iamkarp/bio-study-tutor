# Haploid (n)

## Summary
A cell with **one copy of each chromosome** (one set, denoted **n**). Human haploid cells have **23 chromosomes**. Gametes (sperm and eggs) are haploid; they fuse at fertilization to produce a [[terms/diploid|diploid]] zygote.
