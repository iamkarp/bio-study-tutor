# Heterozygous

## Summary
Carrying **one dominant and one recessive allele** for a given gene (e.g. Yy). Heterozygotes can be **[[terms/carrier|carriers]]** of recessive disease alleles — they don't show the disease but can pass the recessive allele to offspring.
