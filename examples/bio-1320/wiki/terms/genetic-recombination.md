# Genetic Recombination

## Summary
The production of **new combinations of alleles** in offspring. The two main sources during meiosis are **[[processes/crossing-over]]** (in prophase I) and **independent assortment** (in metaphase I). Together, they ensure that no two gametes from a single individual are identical (apart from rare exceptions).
