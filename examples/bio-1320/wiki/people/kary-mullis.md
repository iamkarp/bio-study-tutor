# Kary Mullis (1944–2019)

## Summary
American biochemist who **invented the polymerase chain reaction (PCR)** in 1983. PCR amplifies DNA exponentially via cycles of heat-driven denaturation, primer annealing, and polymerase extension. Mullis won the **Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1993** for the discovery. PCR is now indispensable in forensics, paternity testing, medical diagnostics, and basic research.
