# Marfan Syndrome

## Summary
An **autosomal-dominant** disorder caused by mutations in the **fibrillin-1 (FBN1) gene**. The textbook example of **[[terms/pleiotropy|pleiotropy]]** — one gene with **multiple, seemingly unrelated effects**:
- Tall stature, long limbs, long fingers (arachnodactyly)
- Lens dislocation in the eye
- Aortic aneurysm and other cardiovascular issues
- Joint hyperflexibility

All these symptoms arise because fibrillin-1 is a connective-tissue protein used throughout the body.
