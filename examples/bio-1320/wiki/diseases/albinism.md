# Albinism

## Summary
An **autosomal-recessive** disorder characterized by lack of melanin pigment in skin, hair, and eyes. Caused by mutations in genes (typically TYR — tyrosinase) that produce or transport melanin. Listed in the chapter as an example of a recessive trait.
