# Alkaptonuria

## Summary
An **autosomal-recessive** metabolic disorder — inability to metabolize homogentisic acid (a tyrosine-pathway intermediate). The acid accumulates and turns urine black on standing. Listed in the chapter as another classic recessive trait.
