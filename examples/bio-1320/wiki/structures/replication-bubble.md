# Replication Bubble

## Summary
The locally unwound region of DNA at an [[structures/origin-of-replication]], with two **[[structures/replication-fork|replication forks]]** moving in opposite directions. Eukaryotes have **many** replication bubbles per chromosome (because there are many origins); they expand outward until adjacent bubbles merge.
