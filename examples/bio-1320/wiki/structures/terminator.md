# Terminator

## Summary
A DNA sequence at the **end of a gene** that signals [[enzymes/rna-polymerase]] to stop transcribing. When RNA polymerase reaches the terminator, it dissociates from the DNA and releases the new RNA. The DNA re-forms a double helix.
