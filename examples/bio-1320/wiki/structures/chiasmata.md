# Chiasmata

## Summary
The **physical sites of [[processes/crossing-over]]** between non-sister chromatids of homologous chromosomes during [[processes/prophase-i]]. Visible as X-shaped structures where chromatids exchange segments. In human cells, each pair of homologues typically forms **2–3 chiasmata**. They also serve a mechanical role: they hold homologues together until anaphase I.
