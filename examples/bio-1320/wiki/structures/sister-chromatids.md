# Sister Chromatids

## Summary
The **two identical copies** of a chromosome produced by [[processes/dna-replication]] in [[processes/s-phase]], held together at the centromere. They are separated during **anaphase of mitosis** or **anaphase II of meiosis**. They are *not* homologous chromosomes — they are exact copies of each other (apart from any rare replication errors).
