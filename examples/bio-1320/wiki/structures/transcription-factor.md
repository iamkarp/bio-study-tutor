# Transcription Factor

## Summary
A **protein that binds to a specific DNA sequence** (often near a [[structures/promoter]] or enhancer) and influences whether a gene is transcribed. In eukaryotes, [[enzymes/rna-polymerase]] **cannot bind a promoter without transcription factors first** — they are required for transcription to begin.

Different cell types have different sets of transcription factors active, which is why a liver cell expresses different genes than a neuron despite both having the same DNA. Transcription factors are central to [[concepts/gene-regulation]].
