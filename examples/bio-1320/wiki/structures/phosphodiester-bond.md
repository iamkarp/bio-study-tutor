# Phosphodiester Bond

## Summary
The covalent bond linking the **3' carbon** of one nucleotide's deoxyribose to the **5' phosphate** of the next nucleotide. Phosphodiester bonds form the backbone of a DNA (or RNA) strand. [[enzymes/dna-polymerase-iii|DNA polymerase III]] catalyzes their formation during replication; [[enzymes/dna-ligase|DNA ligase]] catalyzes the closure of the final phosphodiester bond between Okazaki fragments.
