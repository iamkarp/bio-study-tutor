# Phosphate Group (PO₄⁻)

## Summary
A negatively charged group attached to the **5' carbon** of [[molecules/deoxyribose]] in a DNA nucleotide. Phosphate groups link to the next nucleotide's 3'-OH via a [[structures/phosphodiester-bond]], building up the [[structures/sugar-phosphate-backbone]]. The negative charges of phosphates make DNA migrate toward the positive electrode in [[techniques/gel-electrophoresis|gel electrophoresis]].
