# Intron

## Summary
**"Intervening" DNA sequences** within a gene that get **transcribed into pre-mRNA but spliced out** before the mRNA leaves the nucleus. **Introns do NOT code for protein.** They do not appear in the mature mRNA, never reach the ribosome, and never get translated. Most eukaryotic genes contain multiple introns interrupting their exons.
