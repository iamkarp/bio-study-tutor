# Exon

## Summary
**"Expressed" DNA sequences** within a gene. Exons **stay in the mature mRNA** after splicing and are translated into protein. Through [[concepts/alternative-splicing]], a single gene's exons can be assembled in different combinations to produce multiple protein isoforms.
