# Y Chromosome

## Summary
The much smaller of the two human sex chromosomes, carrying very few genes (most importantly the SRY gene that triggers male development). Present **only in males (XY)**. Father determines sex of offspring — he passes either X (→ daughter) or Y (→ son).
