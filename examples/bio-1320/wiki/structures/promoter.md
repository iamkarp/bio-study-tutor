# Promoter

## Summary
A specific DNA sequence at the **start of a gene** that serves as the binding site for [[enzymes/rna-polymerase]]. In eukaryotes, [[structures/transcription-factor|transcription factors]] bind the promoter first and recruit RNA polymerase. The promoter does not get transcribed — it just signals "transcription starts here."
